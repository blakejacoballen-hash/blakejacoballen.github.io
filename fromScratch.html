Why This Interests Me

Wildland firefighting combines endurance, discipline, leadership, and service. It requires physical readiness, mental toughness, and the ability to work as part of a team in unpredictable environments.

Preparation Priorities

Training for wildland fire involves building endurance, leg strength, work capacity, and familiarity with hiking under load.

  1. Physical Preparation
    • Zone 2 cardio
    • Pack hikes
    • Leg strength training
    • Core endurance
  2. Gear Readiness
    • Boots broken in
    • Quality socks and liners
    • Hydration system
    • Work gloves and clothing layers
  3. Mindset
    • Team-first attitude
    • Consistency
    • Attention to detail
    • Willingness to learn
